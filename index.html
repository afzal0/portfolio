Â About Me

My journey into the world of Data Science and Geoinformatics began during my undergraduate studies in Geography at Delhi University. I was captivated by the analytical aspects of spatial data and the potential it held for solving complex real-world problems. This fascination led me to pursue a Master of Science in Geoinformatics at TERI School of Advanced Studies, where I delved deeper into GIS tools and remote sensing techniques, combining technical skills with my passion for data-driven insights. My master's thesis focused on predicting the spread of Influenza A and B using a spatial Bayesian distributed lag nonlinear model, allowing me to explore the intersection of epidemiology and spatial data analysis to address public health challenges.
Seeking to expand my expertise, I enrolled in a Master of Data Science program at Monash University. Here, I immersed myself in advanced data analysis, machine learning, and cloud computing, realizing that I could merge my logical and creative thinking to develop innovative data solutions. My role as an Associate Software Engineer at Precisely enabled me to apply these skills in a professional setting, where I contributed to developing and enhancing data products that support business growth and improve operational efficiency.
Recognized with the Sir John Monash Scholarship for Excellence and multiple performance awards, I am committed to continuous learning and professional development. My technical proficiency includes programming languages such as Python, R, and Java, along with expertise in AWS, SQL, MongoDB, Tableau, Power BI, and PySpark. Additionally, I am adept at using GIS tools like ArcGIS Pro and QGIS to deliver comprehensive geospatial solutions.
I am passionate about utilizing data to drive meaningful improvements in healthcare and other sectors, aiming to contribute to projects that enhance patient outcomes and operational efficiencies. With a collaborative spirit and meticulous attention to detail, I am eager to embrace new challenges and make a significant impact in the data engineering landscape.
